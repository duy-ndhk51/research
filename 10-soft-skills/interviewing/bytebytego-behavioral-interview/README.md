## Course-Level Mental Model

Behavioral interviews evaluate past behavior as evidence for future operating level. The goal is to choose truthful stories that show both fit and level for the role, not to invent a polished persona.

Strong preparation means building a story bank, mapping each story to competencies, and calibrating each story against what the company values.
