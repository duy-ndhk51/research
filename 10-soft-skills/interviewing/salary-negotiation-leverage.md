## Negotiation Tips to Remember

1. **Never give the first number** if you can avoid it — let them anchor first
2. **Frame everything as value delivered**, not "I need more money"
3. **Use data**: market comparisons, your impact metrics, team feedback
4. **Negotiate the whole package**: base salary, bonus, equity, learning budget, remote flexibility, title
5. **Timing matters**: negotiate after a successful delivery, positive performance review, or when you have leverage (competing offer)
6. **Be collaborative, not adversarial**: "I want to find something that works for both of us"
7. **Silence is powerful**: after making your ask, stop talking and wait
